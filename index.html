Global Suicide Rates Report

Suicide is the act of intentionally causing one's own death. Mental disorders, physical disorders (such as chronic fatigue syndrome), and substance abuse are risk factors. Some suicides are impulsive acts due to stress, relationship problems, or harassment and bullying. Those who have previously attempted suicide are at a higher risk for future attempts. Although crisis hotlines are common resources, their effectiveness has not been well studied.
